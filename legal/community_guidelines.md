# Community Guidelines

*Last updated: [date]*

These guidelines help keep Backyard a welcoming place for everyone.

## Be Kind

Treat others the way you'd like to be treated. Disagreement is fine — hostility is not.

## No Harassment

Do not target, bully, or intimidate other users. This includes repeated unwanted contact, threats, and dogpiling.

## No Hate Speech

Content that promotes hatred or violence against people based on race, ethnicity, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or other protected characteristics is not allowed.

## No Spam

Do not post repetitive, unsolicited, or automated content. Do not use the service primarily for advertising.

## No Illegal Content

Do not post content that is illegal in the jurisdiction where this instance is operated. This includes but is not limited to CSAM, which will result in immediate and permanent removal.

## Respect Privacy

Do not share other people's personal information without their consent. Do not post private conversations or media without permission.

## Content Warnings

Use content warnings for posts containing sensitive material such as graphic imagery, discussions of violence, or other potentially distressing content.

## Reporting

If you see content that violates these guidelines, report it to the instance administrators. We will review reports and take appropriate action.

## Enforcement

Violations may result in content removal, temporary restrictions, or permanent bans at the administrators' discretion. You will be notified if action is taken against your account.
